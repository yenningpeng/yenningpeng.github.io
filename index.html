About

— · —

大學畢業於工業產品設計學系，擅長極簡的風格刻劃描繪，以簡單造型呈現物體的質感。曾於軟體前端開發、紙模型禮品、印刷產業服務。對產品從設計到量產各階段皆有參與經驗。

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Product Design, specializing in minimalist design with a focus on expressing materiality through clean, simple forms. My professional experience spans front-end software development, paper model gift design, and the printing industry. Throughout my career, I have been involved in every stage of the product development process, from concept and design to production and mass manufacturing.
